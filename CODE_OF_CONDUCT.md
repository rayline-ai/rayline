# Code of Conduct

Rayline Local's issue tracker is for reproducible bug reports, feature
requests, security-safe discussion, and maintainer coordination.

Be respectful, direct, and constructive. Do not harass other participants,
post abusive content, publish private information, or include secrets, tokens,
private prompts, customer data, or unsanitized logs.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or close comments and issues that are off-topic,
unsafe, abusive, or not actionable. Repeated abuse may lead to a ban from the
project.

Security vulnerabilities must not be reported through public issues. See
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for the private reporting process.
